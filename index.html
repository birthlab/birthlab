Lab for Brain Imaging Research at Tsinghua (BIRTH)

                                  清华大学脑影像实验室

The lab for Brain Imaging Research at Tsinghua (BIRTH) is a part of the Center for Biomedical Imaging
Research at Tsinghua and the Beijing Multimodal Medical Imaging Engineering Technology Research
Center of the Biomedical Engineering Department within the School of Medicine at Tsinghua University.

Our research lies at the interface of biomedical imaging, brain imaging, neuroscience, computer vision,
machine learning and focuses on the development and translation of cutting-edge human neuroimaging
methods especially using magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and artificial intelligence for advancing the
characterization and understanding of brain in health and diseases as well as improving patient care.
